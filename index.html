I am a Principal Research Scientist at Technicolor Research Center in Los Altos, CA. My current research focuses on data privacy and personalization. My work leverages techniques from information theory, random matrix theory, statistics and probability theory, and aims at bridging theory and practice.

My research interests include:

  • balancing personalization and privacy needs: privacy-utility tradeoffs
  • user behavior and interest modelling
  • privacy against inference attacks: from theory to practice
  • information-theoretic privacy
  • differential privacy
  • privacy for time-series, including smart-meter privacy
